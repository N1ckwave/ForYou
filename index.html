September 21, 2025
Hey,

I've been sitting with this for a while, trying to figure out whether to say it or just leave it. I decided to say it, because not saying it feels dishonest, and you deserve honesty more than you deserve me being comfortable.

I miss you. Not in a way I can easily explain or put away. Just — I think about our time together more than I probably should, and I find myself going back to September 21st a lot. That day meant something real to me. What came after did too.

I'm not writing this to make things complicated or to pressure you into anything. I just couldn't keep pretending I was completely fine with how things ended. I wasn't. I'm still not, if I'm being straight with you.

You made things feel different when you were around. Lighter, somehow. I don't think you fully know what that was worth to me — but it was worth a lot.

I don't know what you're thinking right now, or what you want. Maybe you've moved on and this is awkward to read. Maybe you haven't. Either way, I needed you to know where I actually stand.

Still yours,
if you'll have me
P.S. — I almost didn't send this. I'm glad I did.
